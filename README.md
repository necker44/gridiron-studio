## Features

- 🎯 **Designer** — Offense and defense formations, draggable players, route drawing
- 🔲 **Lineman Studio** — Full OL vs DL blocking scheme designer
- ✏️ **Drawing Board** — Freehand whiteboard for quick diagrams
- 📋 **Playbook** — Save and reload plays across sessions
- ▶ **Play animation** — Watch all players run their routes simultaneously
- 📱 **Works on iPhone/iPad** — Open in Safari, add to home screen

## On your iPhone

1. Open the link above in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**

It will appear as an app icon on your home screen.
